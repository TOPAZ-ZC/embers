the mark

The flame.

An inline SVG — not an emoji, not an image. Two-path: ember flame on a log base. Ships with a hard drop-shadow that matches the rest of the system.

Paired with the wordmark in Fraunces italic, SOFT 100, WONK 1. Minimum clear-space equals the flame's height. Use 🔥 emoji only in body copy, never as the mark.

the voice (copy)

How it talks.

Warm. Direct. Specific over generic. Matches the founder's texting voice — which is the product. No AI-PR language. No consultant mush.

Do

  • Start headings with verbs or specific claims
  • Use second person: "your dad," "your top lead"
  • Lead with concrete emotional specifics — brisket, food, family, lead going cold
  • Acknowledge the weird thing head-on
  • Short paragraphs. One or two sentences. Then break.
  • Fraunces italic on the one word that carries the emotion

Don't

  • No "revolutionary," "seamless," "AI-powered," "next-generation"
  • No iMessage blue. Ever.
  • No stock photos of smiling families
  • No hedging — "sort of" and "kind of" die
  • No multi-paragraph explanations. Cut harder.
  • No 🔥 emoji in the logo slot — use the SVG
the legibility

Accessibility.

WCAG AA across the palette. Chunky borders help low-vision and motion-sensitive users alike. Native HTML, no ARIA gymnastics.

Contrast ratios

night on cream : 13.1:1 (AAA)

night-soft on cream : 5.4:1 (AA)

marshmallow on ember-deep : 4.9:1 (AA)

marshmallow on night : 16.8:1 (AAA)

Motion + focus

Sparks animation respects prefers-reduced-motion — disable when requested.

Focus: 3px dashed outline in ember-deep, 4px offset.

All interactives keyboard-reachable. No div-as-button. Native <details> for FAQ.
